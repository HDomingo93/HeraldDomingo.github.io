About Me

I have always pondered the idea of becoming a data analyst but I felt overwhelmed on where to begin my journey whilst working as a Physiotherapy Assistant for Bupa. I finally started my data journey and completed a Level 3 Data Technician Apprenticeship provided by Corndel. I have developed a passion for data, collecting, analysing and telling its story to different audiences. Currently possessing 8+ years experience in the healthcare industry, I am aiming to change my career into data analysis.

To some people, cleaning data sets can be a daunting experience but I enjoy on taking the challenge using different tools such as Microsoft Excel and Power Query when cleaning data. I have also automated my cleansing steps by using VBA or Power query. I am able to produce visuals and dashboards using Microsoft Power Bi to tell the story of the data which one of my dashboard for my Bupa clinics appointments analysis was deemed useful by one of Bupa's Clinic Sales Manager. Other skills I have learnt include: SQL, data modelling and administrative skills. I have started learning Microsoft SQL Server recently and I still want to enthusiastically learn other programming languages such as Python and R.
